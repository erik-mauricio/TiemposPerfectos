    Bienvenidos a Tiempos Perfectos!

    Que es TiemposPerfectos?

    TiemposPerfectos is a web application to stregthen students' Spanish skills. Learning a new language is hard, but the only way to improve is hacer mas practica.

    Reading Comp

    On the Reading Comprehension, users can generate a body of text at a certain difficulty. Then answer a mix of MCQ and open ended questions.

    Conversations

    On the Conversations page, users will practice real time speaking skills with AI

    Grammar

    The grammar page is a simple and efficient way to get your practice in. Users can select from a drop-down menu, the verb tense they would like to the practice. Next, users will enter how many practice problems they would like to have. Aftewards, there will generated questions for the users to practice conjugating their verbs
